# Owlyfi Referral System

The Owlyfi Referral System enables you to earn rewards by inviting other traders. It features a transparent, multi-tier structure offering both direct (L1) and indirect (L2) commissions, as well as trading fee discounts for the users you invite. Commissions are settled **every hour**.

## Referral Tiers & Rewards

Your referral tier is automatically determined by the total trading fees (commissions) generated by the users you have directly invited (L1). As their generated fees grow, your tier upgrades, unlocking higher commission rates, larger invite caps, and better discounts for your referees.

Commissions are calculated based on the standard platform fee (5.5 bps).

| Tier | Min. Fee Referred | L1 Reward | L2 Reward | Discount | Invites |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Normal** | $0 | **30%** | **2%** | **2%** | 5 |
| **Advanced** | ≥ $10 | **35%** | **3%** | **3%** | 20 |
| **VIP** | ≥ $500 | **40%** | **5%** | **5%** | **Unlimited** |

<Note>
The referral system is currently in its early stages. Owly reserves the right to adjust reward ratios and rules to ensure the long-term sustainability of the platform.
</Note>

## How Referee Discounts Work

When a new user signs up using your referral code, they receive a **trading fee discount** for their first **30 days**.

- The discount rate depends on your tier at the time of their registration (e.g., 5% to 15% fee reduction).
- **Automatic application**: Discounts are directly applied to the trading fees on-chain. Referees do not need to manually claim any discount rebates; they simply pay lower fees upfront.

## Referral Attribution

Owly follows a **First-Touch Attribution** policy to ensure fair rewards for the original referrer:

*   **Logic**: A user's referrer is determined by the **first referral link** they click.
*   **Persistent Binding**: Once a user has visited Owly via a referral link, that referrer's code is "locked in" for that user.
*   **No Overwriting**: Even if the user subsequently clicks a referral link from a different person, they will still be registered under the original referrer upon signing up.

## Earnings & Settlement

### Hourly Settlement
Referral commissions are calculated and settled **every hour**. The system snapshots trading activity and distributes rewards to your referral balance. Note that commissions are calculated on the full platform fee rate, unaffected by the discount granted to the referee.

### Withdrawals
1. Navigate to your Referral Dashboard.
2. Ensure your available balance is at least **$1** (minimum withdrawal).
3. Submit a withdrawal request.
4. **Processing**: Your withdrawal goes into a `pending` state for security review. Once approved by our team, the funds will be batch-transferred directly to your on-chain wallet address via Hyperliquid.

> Minimum withdrawal amount: **$1**

## How It Works

Suppose you are at **Normal** tier and invite **Alice** (L1), who in turn invites **Bob** (L2).

In a given hour, Alice and Bob each generate $10,000 in trading volume (platform fee 5.5 bps each):

| | Calculation | Amount |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Fee from Alice (L1) | $10,000 × 5.5 bps | $5.50 |
| Fee from Bob (L2) | $10,000 × 5.5 bps | $5.50 |
| Your L1 reward from Alice (30%) | $5.50 × 30% | $1.65 |
| Your L2 reward from Bob (2%) | $5.50 × 2% | $0.11 |
| Alice's discount (2%) | Applied on-chain automatically | fee reduced by 2% |
| **Your total reward** | $1.65 + $0.11 | **$1.76** |

> Rewards accumulate in your referral balance every hour and can be withdrawn once you reach the $1 minimum.

## Anti-Fraud & Eligibility

To ensure fairness and prevent manipulation:
- Self-referrals (using multiple accounts owned by the same entity) are strictly prohibited and will be detected by our system.
- Standard anti-sybil checks apply to validate the authenticity of invited users.
- Accounts found violating the referral rules may have their referral status invalidated and rewards forfeited.
