Owly serves two primary user groups, each with unique benefits and opportunities.

---

## Followers

<Card title="For Followers" icon="user-plus" color="#7C3AED">
  Individuals who want to grow their portfolio by automatically copying successful traders.
</Card>

### Why Become a Follower?

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Passive Income" icon="coins">
    Let expert traders manage your positions while you focus on other things.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Learn by Doing" icon="graduation-cap">
    Observe real trading strategies in action to improve your own skills.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Diversification" icon="chart-pie">
    Follow multiple traders to spread risk across different strategies.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Ideal for:

- Busy professionals who lack time for active trading
- Beginners who want to learn from experienced traders
- Investors seeking exposure to crypto derivatives without deep expertise

---

## Curators

<Card title="For Curators" icon="crown" color="#F59E0B">
  Skilled traders who want to build a following and earn additional income from their expertise.
</Card>

### Why Become a Curator?

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Earn Performance Fees" icon="dollar-sign">
    Receive a percentage of profits generated for your followers.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Build Reputation" icon="star">
    Establish yourself as a top trader with verified on-chain performance.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Scale Your Impact" icon="chart-line">
    Amplify your trading edge by managing more capital through followers.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Ideal for:

- Professional traders with consistent track records
- Fund managers looking to expand their reach
- Trading educators who want to demonstrate live performance
