## Overview

**Owly** is the ultimate asset management layer on [Hyperliquid](https://hyperliquid.xyz), featuring AI-powered copy trading and automated strategies.

  <Card icon="users" href="/introduction/who-is-owly-for#followers" title="For Followers">

## Core Mission

We believe that access to sophisticated trading strategies should not be limited to professional traders. Owly democratizes trading by:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Discover">
    Browse and analyze performance data of verified traders on the platform.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect">
    Link your Hyperliquid sub-account to follow your chosen traders.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Grow">
    Watch your portfolio grow as trades are automatically mirrored in real-time.
  </Step>
</Steps>
