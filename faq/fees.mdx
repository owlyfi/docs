Owly maintains a simple and transparent fee structure. We only charge a small fee on your trades to sustain the platform, with pricing based on the strategy you use.

## Fee Summary

<CardGroup cols={1}>
  <Card title="Platform Fee" icon="receipt">
    Copy trading is charged **0.055%** (5.5 bps) on transaction volume. Other trading bots are charged **0.02%** (2 bps) on transaction volume.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Detailed Breakdown

| Fee Type | Rate | When Applied |
|----------|------|--------------|
| **Copy Trading Fee** | 0.055% | Per copy trade executed |
| **Other Trading Bot Fee** | 0.02% | Per bot trade executed |
| **Performance Fee** | 0% | No profit sharing |
| **Management Fee** | 0% | No hidden costs |
| **Withdrawal Fee** | 0% | Network gas only |

## No Performance Fees

Unlike other platforms, Owly does **not** charge any performance fees. You keep 100% of your trading profits. The only platform trading cost is **0.055%** for copy trading or **0.02%** for other trading bots, depending on the strategy you use.

## No Hidden Fees

Owly does not charge:
- ❌ Account opening fees
- ❌ Monthly subscription fees
- ❌ Deposit fees
- ❌ Inactivity fees

<Tip>
  Always check a curator's fee percentage before starting copy trading. Higher fees require proportionally higher returns to be worthwhile.
</Tip>
